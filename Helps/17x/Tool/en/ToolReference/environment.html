Environment¶

Overview¶

This panel specifies the environment, such as background and post effects in the scene. It is for checking the appearance of effects when the environment changes.

Parameter¶

Background Color¶

Specify the color of the background.

Background image¶

Specify an image for the background.

Ground¶

Show a ground surface. It is useful for checking soft particles or depth.

Bloom¶

Specify parameters of bloom.

Tonemap¶

Specify a kind of tone maps.
